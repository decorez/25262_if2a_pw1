About Our Company

  • Founded in 1972.
  • Employs 500 staffs in the network of 22 branches across Indonesia.
  • Consistently in the past 21 years named Top Agents for major carriers.
  • Recipient of Best Performance Award from ABACUS.
  • First Travel Agent listed in the Jakarta Stock Exchange in 1989.
  • First Travel Agent with Customer Relationship Management (CRM).

Scope of Services

Online System Airlines Companies

At the moment, we are implementing two main systems in our online computer systems, such as : SABRE. As to stock of tickets, we always
keep tickets in good and up-to-date stock at our Head Office as well as our branch offices, thanks to the good long standing relationship we
maintain so well with airlines concerned, and in that way ticket stock exhaustion are fully watched out and always avoided as replenishment
continuously take place before exhaustion happens.

Being an industry leader, TravelOkay able to provide its customers with the most effective and competitive airfares applicable. Consistently
named Top Agent by major Domestic and International carriers in the past 25 years, like Garuda Indonesia, Singapore Airlines, Cathay Pacific
Airways, British Airways, Qantas, Lufthansa, Air France, KLM Royal Dutch Airlines, Northwest, China Airlines, Eva Air, Malaysian Airlines, Thai
Airways, Lion Air, etc.

TravelOkay is also the recipient of Abacus Award (Computerized Reservations System) for Best Performance Agent.

Human Resources

Our human resources form a solid asset to the company providing well trained and professional staff for any service they have to render to
their clients, ranging from ticketing, hotel reservations, incentive and just leisure tours, etc. They are ready and behind them there are always
the handy skill and eagerness to serve, to offer the best and above all the convenience and satisfaction of their clients regarding their travel.
